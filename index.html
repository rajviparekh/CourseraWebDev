Three meals of the day

BREAKFAST

Breakfast is the first meal of the day. It is consumed in the morning. It is supposed to be the heaviest meal of the day. It consists of fruits, juices, carbs, proteins.

LUNCH

Lunch is the second meal of the day. It is consumed after 12pm. It is less heavier than breakfast and consists of usually vegetables, proteins, fats, carbs.

DINNER

Dinner is the last meal of the day. It is usually lighter than all the other meals because it is consumed before sleeping. It is again a combination of protein, fats, carbs, vegetables etc.
